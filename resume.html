About me

Presentation

small presentation
I am Ciortan Andrei, a passionate individual about computer science since a very young age. The intriguing world of computers and technology has always captivated my attention and fueled my thirst for knowledge. Ever since I laid my hands on my first computer, I have been enchanted by the opportunity to explore the infinite digital frontier. My passion for computer science has grown alongside me, becoming an integral part of my identity. I am fascinated by the beauty of a well-designed algorithm and the power it holds to solve complex problems with just a few lines of code. However, my passion extends beyond just coding. I am captivated by the potential of artificial intelligence and machine learning, the interaction between humans and machines, and the creation of innovative solutions that enhance people's lives.

Why did I choose ITI

why this school?
I chose to attend ITI Galileo Galilei because it was the only school where I found the perfect combination to nurture my passions and learn what I truly love. From the moment I started my education, I knew that this school would be the ideal place to unleash my curiosity and thirst for knowledge in the field of computer science. What immediately struck me about ITI Galileo Galilei was the emphasis placed on computer science and technology as fundamental disciplines. Moreover, ITI Galileo Galilei fostered a collaborative learning environment where I could share ideas and projects with equally passionate classmates. We worked together on stimulating projects, putting into practice what we had learned and developing our teamwork and problem-solving skills.

Next step

university or work?
For the upcoming year, I have decided to continue my studies at the University of Computer Science in Genoa because I believe it will provide me with a solid foundation for my future. After dedicating so much effort and passion to computer science throughout my educational journey, I feel it is time to further deepen my knowledge and acquire more advanced skills. My ultimate goal is to secure a solid and fulfilling job in the field of computer science, an industry that continues to evolve rapidly. I am aware that technological advancements can bring changes to the job market, but I believe a strong university education is crucial to adapt and thrive in this ever-changing environment. dream of finding employment that allows me to apply my computer science skills, tackle exciting challenges, and contribute to technological progress. I aspire to work in a dynamic and collaborative environment where I can continue to learn and grow professionally.

other interests
